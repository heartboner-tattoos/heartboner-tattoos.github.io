Meet the Artist!


Hi! I’m Heart Boner, I’m a Spanish tattoo artist currently based in Higashi-Hiroshima.
Before moving to Japan, I spent the last years working as an artist in Czech Republic and Austria.

I love creating unique, unusual, and creative pieces!
I have a variety of original flash designs ready for you to pick. If you’re new to tattoos, don’t worry! I’ll guide you every step of the way.I hold an official tattooing certification from Europe, maintain strict hygiene standards, and ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for you.

My studio is located about an hour from Hiroshima Station, near the famous sake city of Saijo.
Feel free to contact me anytime for a consultation or to book an appointment!
